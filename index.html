About Me

As a versatile web developer and designer, I possess a range of technical and creative skills that help me craft seamless, responsive, and dynamic websites. My expertise spans both front-end and back-end development, allowing me to tackle the full web development process.

Front-End Development: I am skilled in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which form the backbone of all my projects. I use Bootstrap to build mobile-first, responsive layouts, while jQuery enhances user interactions with smooth animations and transitions. My proficiency in these tools ensures that I can create user-friendly interfaces that are both visually appealing and functional.

Back-End Development: On the back-end, I specialize in PHP and MySQL, developing robust databases and dynamic web applications that scale efficiently. Whether it's creating a custom content management system or integrating external APIs, I ensure the back-end architecture supports a smooth user experience.

WordPress and CMS: I am particularly proficient in WordPress, where I build custom themes and plugins to suit specific client needs. From simple business sites to full-scale eCommerce solutions, my work in WordPress ensures that the end product is highly customizable and easy to manage.

Design and Tools: While my technical abilities are strong, my design skills also play a crucial role in creating aesthetically pleasing websites. Using Figma, I design intuitive and user-friendly interfaces that ensure a smooth browsing experience. This combination of design and development sets my work apart, allowing me to create websites that are not only functional but also visually engaging.

By combining my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, WordPress, PHP, MySQL, and Figma, I strive to deliver high-quality websites that meet both user needs and business goals. My goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of web design and development to deliver impactful digital experiences.
